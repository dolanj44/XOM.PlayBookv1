Use this PlayBook to establish your DevOps best practice.

The PlayBook provides a way for project teams at Exxon to assess themselves in DevOps practices and create a learning plan.

Assess Yourself Here

How this PlayBook app works

  • This app was created with the intent of providing a spot for project teams to self-assess their DevOps practices.
  • The steps you should follow are below:
  • Perform an Agile\DevOps Self Assessment
  • Fill out your committment playbook
  • Utilize the custom made guide to help you bootstrap your application to be up and runnig as quickly as possible

Next Steps

  • If you are following the Unity style guide, then you are well on your way to making consistent looking web applications in the most efficient manner.
    • What the PlayBook intends to do is take what Unity provided for the front end and allow you to get established best practices for DevOps on your team.
